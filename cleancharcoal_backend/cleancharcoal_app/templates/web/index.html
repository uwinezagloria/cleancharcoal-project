Who We Are

We are a passionate team of young innovators dedicated to transforming the traditional charcoal process into a cleaner, safer, and more efficient system. By combining embedded sensors, smart technology, and community-driven tools, we aim to protect forests, reduce harmful smoke, and support local livelihoods.

Services We Provide

Sensors Monitor

Embedded sensors inside the charcoal kiln track temperature, smoke, and humidity in real time. This ensures the burning process stays efficient and safe.

Alerts & Recommendations

The system provides instant alerts if conditions are unsafe or if adjustments are needed, helping prevent accidents and reduce smoke.

Permission Requests

Citizens can easily request permission to burn charcoal through our platform. Community leaders review each request to ensure it is legal, fair, and environmentally responsible.

Data Sent to Dashboard
